About Me

I am a person who measures life through the lens of morality. I am endlessly curious, always seeking to learn something new and absorb it deeply, allowing it to shape my understanding of myself and the world around me. I am drawn to the warmth of human compassion, brotherhood, and the tenderness shared between all living beings.

To me, all religions hold equal value, and I firmly believe that every faith is guided by the principles of morality. Hatred or intolerance toward others in the name of one's own religion is unacceptable. True liberation, I believe, lies in secular harmony and universal empathy.

I strive to live with integrity, wishing well not only for myself and my family but for all humanity. My journey is about growth, understanding, and contributing positively to the world around me.
